Self Driving Cars

Our world gets more and more automated every day. Lots of work that was done by humans will now be done by fully automated machines. So, it´s not surprising that the automation is getting more important than ever. In the car industry we haven’t found a solution that could replace human use in our traffic. But the car industry is developing fast. Most of the new cars have got an assistant that can help you in the daily traffic. For Example, the Intelligent Speed Adaption, this assistant can regulate your speed, or the Lane departure system which keeps you on the right lane. So, you can see that there is only a small step to a fully automated car. But when this stage is reached our everyday life will never be the same.
